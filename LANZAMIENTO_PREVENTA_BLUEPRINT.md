### §5.2 Standard SKU — $150-300K MXN per launch
For full-size launches (single-tower 50-200 unit, or master-planned $50M+ project). Same scope, larger ad budget, more AEO content, dedicated launch team.
- **50% on signing**
- **30% on day 45** (proof point: leads flowing, AI live)

### §5.3 Continuation tiers (after the 90-day test)
Pravin asked specifically about "what's the cost going forward." Three paths:

| Tier | Monthly fee | What's included |
|---|---|---|
| **Full continuation** | $15-25K MXN/mo | Full ad management + Sofia AI + AEO maintenance + weekly reports. Discounted because the system is built. |

### §5.4 Ad spend (separate from agency fee)
Always disclosed as separate from agency fee. Mexican developers buy "managed services" — the fee is for management, the ad budget is theirs.

| Project type | Recommended monthly ad spend | Expected qualified leads/mo |
|---|---|---|
| Small launch (test) | $30-50K MXN | 30-60 |
| Mid-size launch | $50-100K MXN | 60-150 |
| Large launch / branded hospitality | $100-200K MXN | 150-300 |

### §5.5 Honest CPL math (anti-bullshit)
Pravin asked: "what's your average budget per lead?"

The answer we give in writing (from the conversation, codified):

> "Anyone who quotes you a tight CPL sight-unseen is guessing. Your real number gets measured in week 2-3 of the launch. Realistic range across pre-sale international-buyer launches: raw lead cost $15-40 USD on Meta + Google. Qualified-lead cost (post-Sofia) $50-150 USD. Site-visit-booking cost $300-800 USD."

Never quote a tighter range than this. Anyone who does loses trust the moment day-30 numbers don't match.

### §5.6 Volume reality check (the Pravin-style anti-pattern)
When a developer says "I want 100 leads/week" — that's 400/month, which is **way too high for a single development**.

Premium real estate where each deal is $300K-$2M USD converts way better with **30-60 qualified leads/month**. Volume without follow-up actually costs deals.

If the dev's sales team can genuinely work 100/week, scale toward aggressive budget. If 30-60 qualified/month is the actual sweet spot, $50K MXN/month ad spend hits it.
