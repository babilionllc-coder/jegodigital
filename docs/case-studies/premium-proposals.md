## 💡 Strategic value

Cotizaciones is JegoDigital's **quality differentiator vs cheap agencies**. Most prospects compare us against $1,500/mo "marketing freelancers" who deliver PDF reports made in PowerPoint. Showing the cotización quality up front:
1. Pre-qualifies prospects who can't afford our pricing (they self-eliminate)
2. Anchors our pricing as "you get what you pay for"
3. Reduces friction during the proposal stage (they've already seen the format)
