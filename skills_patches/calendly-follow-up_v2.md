## §2 — Calendly event description

**OLD:**
> 30-min strategy call with Alex. We'll review your business, identify gaps, and walk through how we'd help you 10× your inbound leads. Includes a free audit. Limited slots — book early.

**NEW:**
> 15-min collaboration chat with Alex (founder of JegoDigital).
>
> **What this is:** an honest conversation. We learn about your business, you ask whatever you want about how we work with clients. If there's a fit, we figure out next steps together. If there's not, we wish you well.
>
> **What this isn't:** a sales demo. There's no pitch deck. There's no "exclusive limited offer".
>
> **Bring:** anything you're curious about — your current marketing, a competitor you admire, a question we should answer for the broader market. We'd rather learn from you than sell to you.
>
> When you grow, we grow.

## §3 — Calendly intake questions

**OLD intake questions:**
1. Name
2. Email
3. Phone
4. Company
5. What's your biggest marketing challenge right now?
6. What's your monthly marketing budget?

**NEW intake questions:**
1. Name
2. Email
3. WhatsApp
4. Company name
5. What's the most interesting thing your team is working on right now? (open-ended, optional)
6. Anything specific you'd like Alex to research before the chat? (open-ended, optional)

Why: Q5 frames their business as interesting (they're the expert in their world). Q6 invites them to set the agenda — Alex does the homework so the call is high-signal. Drops the budget question entirely from the intake form (it's pricing, never in writing per HR-0).

## §4 — Confirmation email tone

**OLD subject:** "✅ Tu llamada con Alex está confirmada"

**NEW subject:** "{firstName}, vamos en {date} — algunas notas"

**OLD body:**
> Tu llamada con Alex Jego está confirmada para [date/time].
>
> Antes de la llamada, prepara: tu mejor competidor, tu meta de leads mensual, tu presupuesto.
>

**NEW body:**
> {firstName},
>
> Confirmado el chat para [date/time]. Va por Google Meet — link adentro de tu calendario.
>
> Para que sea high-signal:
>
> - **No traigas pitch deck preparado.** Cuéntame de tu equipo en tus palabras.
> - **Trae 1 pregunta honesta.** Algo que dudes del mercado, de un competidor, de una decisión que tienes pendiente. Si yo no sé, te lo digo.
> - **Trae 1 cliente o lead que recuerdes con cariño.** Suelo aprender más de los buenos casos que de los problemáticos.
>
> Si necesitas mover el horario, sin culpa: [reschedule link]. Si surgió algo y no puedes ir, también está bien — escríbeme un WhatsApp y reagendamos cuando puedas.
>
> Alex / JegoDigital
